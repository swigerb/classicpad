# Code of Conduct

ClassicPad follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1. By participating you agree to uphold its principles:

- Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate in all interactions.
- Provide constructive feedback; focus on behavior, not people.
- Accept guidance from maintainers and honor moderation decisions.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers monitor communication channels and will respond to reports of unacceptable behavior. To report an incident, email the repository owner or open a private GitHub discussion with as much detail as possible.

Repeated or serious violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

Thank you for helping keep the ClassicPad community welcoming to everyone.
